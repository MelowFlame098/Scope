## PART 2 — Advanced Financial Ratios (Calculated)

### A. Valuation & Yield Ratios
1. Earnings Yield = 1 / P/E
2. Forward Earnings Yield = 1 / Forward P/E
3. Free Cash Flow Yield = Free Cash Flow / Market Capitalization
4. Operating Cash Flow Yield = Operating Cash Flow / Market Capitalization
5. EBITDA Yield = EBITDA / Enterprise Value
6. Revenue Yield = Revenue / Enterprise Value
7. Book-to-Market Ratio = Book Value / Market Capitalization
8. Price-to-Growth Adjusted Yield = Earnings Yield / Growth Rate

### B. Profitability & Efficiency Ratios
9. Asset Turnover = Revenue / Total Assets
10. Operating Efficiency = Operating Income / Revenue
11. Return on Invested Capital (ROIC) = NOPAT / Invested Capital
12. Cash Return on Invested Capital (CROIC) = Free Cash Flow / Invested Capital
13. Gross Profit Efficiency = Gross Profit / Assets

### C. Growth & Quality Ratios
14. Sustainable Growth Rate (SGR) = ROE × (1 − Dividend Payout Ratio)
15. Earnings Growth Efficiency = EPS Growth / PEG
16. Revenue-to-Earnings Growth Ratio = Revenue Growth / EPS Growth
17. Cash Conversion Ratio = Operating Cash Flow / Net Income
18. Free Cash Flow Conversion = Free Cash Flow / Net Income

### D. Leverage & Risk Ratios
19. Net Debt to EBITDA = (Total Debt − Cash) / EBITDA
20. Debt Service Ratio = Operating Cash Flow / Total Debt
21. Financial Leverage Ratio = Total Assets / Equity
22. Leverage Adjusted Volatility = Beta × Debt to Equity
23. Liquidity Cushion = Cash / Total Debt

### E. Shareholder Return & Capital Allocation
24. Shareholder Yield = Dividend Yield + Buyback Yield
25. Retention Ratio = 1 − Dividend Payout Ratio
26. Reinvestment Rate = (Capital Expenditure) / Operating Cash Flow
27. Capital Efficiency = Revenue Growth / Capital Investment

### F. Ownership & Smart Money Ratios
28. Insider Buying Intensity = Insider Purchases / Shares Outstanding
29. Institutional Accumulation Score = Change in Institutional Ownership
30. Float Turnover Ratio = Volume / Float

### G. Liquidity & Stability
31. Volatility-to-Liquidity Ratio = ATR / Average Volume
32. Turnover Stability = Average Volume / Shares Outstanding

### H. Composite Factor Models
33. Value Score = Weighted(Earnings Yield, FCF Yield, Book-to-Market)
34. Quality Score = Weighted(ROE, Margins, Debt Ratios)
35. Growth Score = Weighted(Revenue Growth, EPS Growth, Forecasts)
36. Low Risk Score = Weighted(Beta, Volatility, Stability)

### I. Risk-Adjusted Metrics
37. Risk-Adjusted Return = Expected Return / Volatility
38. Fundamental Risk Score = Debt + Volatility + Earnings Stability
